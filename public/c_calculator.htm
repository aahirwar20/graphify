Instructions

To calculate the sine of 55 degrees, follow these steps:

  1. Turn on your calculator.
  2. Make sure the calculator is in "degree" mode.
  3. Locate the "sin" function on your calculator.
  4. Enter the value 55.
  5. Press the "sin" button to calculate.
  6. Your calculator will display the result of sin(55).

To calculate 2 raised to the power of 6, follow these steps:

  1. Turn on your calculator.
  2. Locate the "^" or "power" button on your calculator.
  3. Enter the base value, which is 2.
  4. Press the "^" or "power" button.
  5. Enter the exponent value, which is 6.
  6. Press the "=" button to calculate.
  7. Your calculator will display the result of 2^6.
